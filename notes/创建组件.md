## [创建组件的命令](https://angular.cn/cli/generate#component)
> ng generate component <组件名> [options]
<br />
简写：ng g c <组件名> [options]

eg:
- ng g c comp1 (在src/app下创建名为comp1的组件)
- ng g c components/comp1 (在src/app/components下创建名为comp1的组件)

## 常用参数
- changeDetection
- displayBlock
- export
- inlineStyle
- inlineTemplate
- skipTests
